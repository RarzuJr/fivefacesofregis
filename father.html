A Father

Hello, world! I am Regis the Father, Regis the Husband, Regis, the Family man. I was raised in New York City and currently reside in Colorado. At the end of 2010, I received this calling to pack my things and move to Colorado. I didn't know anyone in Colorado at the time, my life was pretty good in New York, and I was not unhappy, but I knew something was missing in my life, and it was in Colorado.

Fast forward eight years later, I am married to the most amazing supportive wife, and I have the coolest son in the world, and they both power me to strive and push to be more successful than I have ever been. Going from being this poor black boy who grew up in the Bronx projects to purchasing my third home in Colorado, I can only credit my family for being there through thick and thin. My superhero powers are unlimited with them by my side, and as long as I have them, this is the core of who I am. .

Accomplishments

  • I bought a one-way ticket to Colorado and built the life I wanted

  • I purchased three homes in Colorado; when growing up, the idea of purchasing a home seemed like an illusion

  • Every two years, I've consistently have been promoted at my job no matter where I've worked
